Hello here is my life


I was born in 1989 in the city of Glasgow I have always been fascinated about design from a young age I spent hours just drawing. I decided at 12 I wanted to become a graphic designer, so I studded to get my advanced Highers in graphic and art and design, I then went on to Glasgow university where I got my degree in graphic design. After graduation I worked with my friends for a year to help build up my portfolio and confidence with the business side of things and of Jan 2016, I began to do self-employed work up until I got my first studio and this is the fruits of my work
